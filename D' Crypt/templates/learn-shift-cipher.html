Shift Cipher


How it Works

Shift cipher is a form of substitution cipher. It replaces each letter in the alphabet by a certain fixed position down and goes round robin.

For a key of 3, the letter 'A' is encrypted to 'D', and the letter 'G' is encrypted to 'J'.
